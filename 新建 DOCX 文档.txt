## Project PRD/Brief: AI-Powered Meeting Summary & Action Item Generator

**Product:** Nexus Project Management Suite
**Feature Name:** AI Meeting Assistant
**Document Version:** 1.0
**Date:** October 26, 2023
**Author:** [Your Name/Product Manager]

---

### 1. Project Overview & Summary

This project aims to introduce an AI-driven feature within the Nexus platform that automatically transcribes meeting audio/video, summarizes key discussions, and extracts actionable tasks. The goal is to significantly reduce the manual effort of post-meeting administration, improve information retention, and enhance team alignment by ensuring critical decisions and tasks are captured and easily accessible.

### 2. Problem Statement

*   **Manual Effort:** Users spend significant time manually summarizing meeting notes, identifying action items, and distributing them to team members. This is often a tedious and error-prone process.
*   **Information Loss:** Key decisions and action items can be missed or forgotten, leading to rework, delays, and poor project outcomes.
*   **Onboarding & Catch-up:** New team members or those who miss meetings struggle to quickly get up to speed on past discussions and context without sifting through extensive raw notes.
*   **Lack of Centralization:** Meeting notes and action items are often scattered across various tools (notepad, email, different apps), making it hard to track and integrate with project tasks.

### 3. Goals & Objectives

*   **Reduce Post-Meeting Admin Time:** Decrease the average time spent on meeting summarization and action item distribution by 30% for active users within 3 months of launch.
*   **Improve Action Item Completion:** Increase the rate of identified action items being linked to Nexus tasks and completed by 15% within 6 months.
*   **Enhance Information Accessibility:** Provide a searchable, centralized repository of meeting summaries and action items linked directly to projects.
*   **Boost User Satisfaction:** Achieve an average user satisfaction score of 4.0/5.0 for the AI Meeting Assistant feature within 6 months of launch.
*   **Drive Engagement:** Increase daily active users (DAU) of the Nexus platform by 5% through improved utility and reduced friction in meeting management.

### 4. Target Audience

*   **Primary:** Project Managers, Team Leads, and Scrum Masters who regularly conduct and manage team meetings.
*   **Secondary:** Individual Contributors who attend meetings and need to track their assigned tasks or catch up on missed discussions.
*   **Tertiary:** Stakeholders and clients who need high-level overviews of project progress and decisions.

### 5. High-Level Features / User Stories

*   **Meeting Recording/Upload:**
    *   As a user, I want to be able to record audio/video directly within a Nexus-scheduled meeting.
    *   As a user, I want to be able to upload a pre-recorded meeting audio/video file (e.g., from Zoom, Teams, Google Meet).
*   **Automated Transcription:**
    *   As a user, I want the system to automatically transcribe the meeting audio/video into text.
*   **AI Summary Generation:**
    *   As a user, I want the system to generate a concise, AI-powered summary of the meeting's key discussion points.
*   **Action Item Extraction:**
    *   As a user, I want the system to automatically identify and list actionable items with potential assignees and due dates from the meeting transcript.
*   **Review & Edit Interface:**
    *   As a user, I want to be able to easily review, edit, and refine the AI-generated summary and action items before sharing.
    *   As a user, I want to be able to manually add or remove action items.
*   **Task Integration:**
    *   As a user, I want to be able to convert extracted action items directly into Nexus tasks, assigning them to team members and setting due dates.
*   **Sharing & Notifications:**
    *   As a user, I want to be able to share the refined summary and action items with meeting participants via Nexus notifications and email.
*   **Meeting History & Search:**
    *   As a user, I want to view all past meeting summaries and their associated tasks within the relevant project or team dashboard.
    *   As a user, I want to be able to search through meeting transcripts and summaries for specific keywords or topics.

### 6. Success Metrics & KPIs

*   **Feature Adoption Rate:** % of active teams/users utilizing the AI Meeting Assistant feature.
*   **Time Savings:** Data from user surveys and estimated time saved on manual note-taking/summarization.
*   **Action Item Conversion Rate:** % of AI-extracted action items converted into Nexus tasks.
*   **Task Completion Rate:** % of tasks originating from AI-extracted action items that are completed on time.
*   **User Satisfaction (CSAT/NPS):** Scores from in-app feedback and dedicated surveys.
*   **Transcription/Summary Accuracy:** Internal metrics on AI model performance (though not directly user-facing).

### 7. Assumptions

*   Users are willing to use an in-platform recording solution or upload meeting files.
*   The chosen AI transcription and summarization services will provide acceptable accuracy and reliability within budget.
*   Data privacy and security concerns related to recording and processing meeting content can be adequately addressed.
*   The current Nexus platform architecture can support the integration of external AI services and handle the data processing load.

### 8. Constraints & Risks

*   **AI Accuracy:** AI models may not always be 100% accurate in transcription, summarization, or action item identification, potentially requiring significant user correction.
*   **Cost:** The operational cost of AI API usage (transcription, NLU) could be substantial, impacting pricing or profitability.
*   **Privacy & Compliance:** Legal and ethical implications of recording and processing potentially sensitive meeting data (e.g., GDPR, CCPA). Clear consent mechanisms will be crucial.
*   **Scalability:** Handling large volumes of audio/video processing and storage could strain infrastructure.
*   **User Resistance:** Some users might be hesitant to trust or rely on AI-generated content.
*   **Integration Complexity:** Integrating with various external meeting platforms (Zoom, Teams etc.) for direct recording/upload might be complex or require specific partner APIs.

### 9. Out of Scope for V1

*   Real-time live transcription and summarization during a meeting.
*   Complex natural language queries on meeting content (e.g., "What was discussed about Q4 budget?").
*   Automated sentiment analysis of meeting discussions.
*   Integration with *all* third-party meeting platforms (focus on major ones first).
*   Multi-language support beyond the primary Nexus language (initially).

### 10. Dependencies

*   Selection and integration of a reliable third-party AI transcription and Natural Language Understanding (NLU) service.
*   Legal and compliance review for data privacy and consent requirements.
*   Existing Nexus user authentication and project/task management modules.
*   Availability of sufficient backend engineering and AI/ML expertise.

### 11. Technical Considerations (High-Level)

*   **Third-Party AI Integration:** RESTful API integration for transcription and summarization services.
*   **Data Storage:** Secure, scalable storage for audio/video files (temporary) and transcribed text/summaries (persistent).
*   **Processing Pipeline:** Asynchronous processing of uploaded/recorded media files.
*   **Security & Privacy:** End-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest. Robust access controls.
*   **Scalability:** Microservices architecture considerations for handling fluctuating processing loads.
*   **UI/UX:** Intuitive interface for managing recordings, reviewing summaries, and converting action items.

### 12. Future Considerations (Post V1)

*   Live transcription and AI assistance during meetings.
*   Advanced analytics on meeting data (e.g., talk time distribution, sentiment trends).
*   Custom vocabulary training for industry-specific jargon.
*   Multi-language support.
*   Integration with more third-party communication tools.
